Update a supplier
=================

Update a supplier in GLPI

1.  Log into GLPI;
2.  Go to the menu Management \> Suppliers ;
3.  Optional: search for a supplier;
4.  Click on the name of the supplier;
5.  Edit fields you want;
6.  Update.
