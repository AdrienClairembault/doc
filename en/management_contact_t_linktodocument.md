Associate a contact to a document
=================================

Associate a contact to a document

1.  Log into GLPI;
2.  Go to the menu Management \> Contacts ;
3.  Optional: search for a contact;
4.  Two possibilities:
    -   1.  Click on the name of the contact
        2.  Go to tab **Documents**
        3.  Select a document or click on **Browse** button to add a new
            one.
        4.  Add

    -   1.  Check the contact
        2.  Massive action: **Add a document**
